WHO WE ARE

19 Events is a technology focused event management company based in Tanzania.

We enable organizations to reach their audiences through executing memorable and engaging events using technology.

Our aim is to inspire solutions and challenge limitation at how organizations connect with their stakeholders. Everything we do is geared towards enabling organizations reach their audience and increase engagement by delivering quality events that attendees will always remember.

We handle all aspects of the event from budgeting, venue search, theme creation, suppliers and vendor management.

Our Services

We provide the following services:

Event Management

We implement traditional event management services including: • Logistics • Event Look and Feel • Event Documentation • Communication

Event Technology Consulting

We implement technologies around: • Participants Engagement • Maximizing Clients ROI • Increasing Participants project experience
